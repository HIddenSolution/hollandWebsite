About

Holland Construction Inc is a family owned and operated company.

The company was founded in 2016 by Dean and Christy Holland. It started as a small remodeling business. Dean was known for his quality work, perfectionism, and critical thinking. He quickly built a loyal clientele base, but in late 2018 Dean was diagnosed with a rare form of brain cancer. We honor his memory by striving to provide top-notch quality work for our customers and give them the best experience possible. Dean's daughter and son in law, Marsha and Sheldon Scott, look over the company in 2020. Sheldon has a lifetime of experience in earth work, running heavy equipment, and farming.


Our main focus is dirt work and hauling for comanche and surrounding counties. We also offer a variety of other services such as land/brush clearing, stock tank construction, and residential/commercial building pads. With our experienced and professional team, we are ready to help you with your next project!

Frequently Asked Questions

If you’ve got a question, we’ve got an answer.

How far do you haul and conduct business? We will haul as far as needed. The price up to 50 miles is calculated by the loaded mile. Mileage exceeding 50 loaded miles is subject to a return-home fee. We do try to use the closest supplier near you to ensure the best price possible.
What if any accidents occur during a job? Holland Construction Inc is properly insured to handle all emergencies and/or accidents that may occur. We take pride in practicing appropriate safety precautions to prevent any accidents from happening.
Do I need a permit to do my dirt wark? If you are inside city limits you may need to check your city's regulations. If you need work on or next to a state highway you will need to contact your nearest TX DOT.
